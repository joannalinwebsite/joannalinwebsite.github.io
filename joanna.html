My Experience at CS4CS

When I first started my time here at CS4CS, I was so confused and I didn't understand anything. I was too scared and embarassed to ask for help because I thought I was the only person that didn't understand a single thing. But I soon realized that I wasn't the only one confused. As time passed, I realized that in order for me to make my time here useful I had to start asking questions about what I was confused about so that I could learn. I realized that me not asking for help and not understanding the material wasn't beneficial to anyone and in fact, it probably did more harm than good. So I started to ask for help and soon, I started to slowly understand the material. I still make mistakes but over time I'll get better hopefully.
